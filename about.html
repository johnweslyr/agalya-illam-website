About Agalya Illam

Our mission is to foster spiritual growth, inner peace, and community well-being through the timeless values of love, service, and self-discipline. We aim to create a harmonious environment where individuals can engage in meditation, yoga, and holistic living, guided by the teachings of our tradition.

In alignment with nature, we also practice sustainable farming and seasonal harvesting. Our ashram encourages organic cultivation methods to support self-sufficiency, healthy living, and respect for the Earth. Through our harvesting plan, we aim to nourish not only the body but also cultivate gratitude and awareness of nature's cycles.

Our Values

  • Service (Seva)
  • Self‑Discipline
  • Sustainability & Harmony with Nature

What We Practice
