Visit & Stay

Accommodation

  • Dormitory Stay
    Shared rooms with basic facilities (Men/Women separate)
  • Private Room
    Single or double occupancy with attached bathroom
  • Eco‑Huts / Cottages
    Limited availability for long‑term or special guests

Facilities Provided

  • Clean bedding and linens
  • Satvik vegetarian meals (3 times a day)
  • Safe drinking water
  • Hot water (limited hours)
  • Wi‑Fi in common areas
  • Laundry (self‑service or paid)

Visitor Guidelines

  • Maintain silence during meditation hours
  • Dress modestly and respectfully
  • Alcohol, tobacco, and non‑veg food are strictly prohibited
  • Participate in seva (voluntary service) if staying more than 2 days
  • Respect ashram rules and fellow residents

Check‑in & Check‑out

Check‑in: 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM
Check‑out: Before 10:00 AM
Prior booking is recommended due to limited space.
